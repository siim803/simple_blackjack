How to play Blackjack

Goal: get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Numbered cards are worth their number, J/Q/K are worth 10, and Aces are worth 1 or 11.

On your turn you can Hit to draw a card or Stand to end your turn. If your hand exceeds 21 you bust and lose. The dealer then reveals their hidden card and draws according to the rules.

How to play this game

  • Hit: draw a card for your hand.
  • Stand: stop drawing and let the dealer play; the result will be shown.
  • Restart: start a new round.
  • Split: a split button is shown but full split functionality is not implemented in this version.
